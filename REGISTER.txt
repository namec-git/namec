Dear User of Namec:

We would like to find out how you are using our software.  Did you
find it useful?  Do you have suggestions for improving name code?

If you use Namec for writing software, please send us a note telling
us what you are doing with it.

We need to know:

  (1) What you are working on - an abstract of your work would be
      fine.

  (2) What version of Namec you use.

  (3) How Namec has helped you, for example, by increasing
      your productivity or allowing you to do things you could not do
      before.  In particular, if you feel that Namec has had a direct 
      bearing on your work, please tell us about this.

We encourage you to cite the use of Namec in your publications.  

To cite Namec, use

  Barber, C.B., "Name code for understandable programs,"
  http://github.com/namec-git/namec/tree/master/doc/pseudocode.rtf, 2018.

Please send e-mail to

    namec-git@qhull.org

Thank you!
